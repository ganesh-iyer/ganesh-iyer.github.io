  • SAP - Project Execution

    Before my graduate studies at Berkeley, I was a User Experience Design Associate at SAP Labs working out of their Bangalore office. I helped ship various SAP enterprise products chief among them being HCM applications and SAP Project Execution. The latter is a product that helps a project manager monitor scheduling, revenue and logistics for a project.

  • PipPop - Exchanging contact information seamlessly

    This project was where my team of 4 took a very grounded UX-research driven approach to designing an interface that helps users exchange contact information in social settings like meetups or career fairs. In the span of 4 months, we performed research, laid out UX guidelines and iteratively prototyped and tested the proof-of-concept at various fidelities.

    View project

  • Visualizing Betting v Performances in Tennis

    In this intense 2-week project, my team designed and prototyped a proof-of-concept for a visualization that allows for comparison between how male tennis players performed on the court and how they were expected to perform. We performed tasks like data cleanup, wireframing, front-end web development and also evaluated the finer visual aspects with users to test our assumptions.

  • Geometris - Creating an embodied and collaborative game for children

    Keeping elementary school children in mind, we attempted to design a different experience of mathematics, one that requires children to use their bodies and to talk to each other in learning basic geometry. This project is special to me because of so many firsts - designing for a physical experience using Arduino, coding the software for a game end-to-end, composing its music among many others!
